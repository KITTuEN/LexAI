Problem Statement: 
Legal procedures and jargons are complex and expensive for common citizens. People struggle to understand their rights, draft simple police complaints, or access basic legal help without costly lawyer fees.

--- Slide 3: Brief about your solution ---
NyayaVyavasth is an advanced AI-powered platform designed to provide accessible initial legal guidance. It acts as a bridge between complex legal language and everyday citizen needs. By using Google Gemini AI, it answers common legal queries, automatically drafts legal complaints, and extracts text from physical legal documents using OCR.

--- Slide 4: Opportunities ---
How different is it from existing ideas?
Traditional platforms only list lawyers. NyayaVyavasth instantly answers legal questions and drafts actual usable legal documents autonomously.

How will it be able to solve the problem?
By giving users a 24/7 AI legal chatbot, ordinary people can understand their rights immediately without paying upfront consultation fees.

USP of the proposed solution:
- Google Gemini powered natural language comprehension for law.
- Instant, automated Document Generation (Complaints/Case drafts).
- OCR Integration to seamlessly digitize and explain physical legal notices.

--- Slide 5: List of features offered by the solution ---
1. AI Chatbot: Instant conversational legal consultations.
2. Automated Document Generation: Creates structured police complaints and case drafts.
3. OCR Integration: Digitizes, reads, and analyzes physical legal documents.
4. Rights Repository: Searchable database of fundamental citizen rights.
5. Lawyer Connect: Connect directly with domain-expert human lawyers (Criminal, Family, Cyber, etc.).

--- Slide 6: Process flow diagram or Use-case diagram ---
```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Citizen User] -->|Login/Register| B{Dashboard}
    B -->|Query / Story| C[AI Legal Chatbot]
    B -->|Upload Document| D[OCR Document Reader]
    B -->|Fill Details| E[Complaint Generator]
    
    C -->|Natural Language Processing| F[Gemini AI Engine]
    D -->|Text Extraction| F
    E -->|Template Filling| F
    
    F -->|Legal Advice / PDF Draft| G[User Output]
    G -->|Optional| H[Connect with Human Lawyer]
```
Use-case Flow:
1. User Registration/Login (JWT Auth based) -> 
2. User selects action (Chat, OCR, Complaint Builder) -> 
3. User talks to Gemini AI / Uploads Document -> 
4. Backend (Flask) processes via AI -> 
5. Output: Simplified Legal Advice / Generated PDF Complaint / Extracted Text.
6. Optional: User reaches out to registered human lawyer for advanced help.

--- Slide 8: Architecture diagram of the proposed solution ---
```mermaid
graph LR
    subgraph Frontend
        A[HTML5/CSS3/JS]
    end
    
    subgraph Backend_Flask
        B[Flask App]
        C[JWT Auth]
        D[Blueprint Routes]
    end
    
    subgraph AI_Layer
        E[Google Gemini AI]
    end
    
    subgraph Database
        F[(MongoDB Atlas)]
    end
    
    A <-->|REST API| B
    B --- C
    B --- D
    D <-->|API Calls| E
    D <-->|Queries| F
```
- Frontend: HTML/CSS/JS (Mobile-friendly web app).
- Backend: Python Flask with Flask-JWT-Extended for security.
- AI Layer: Google Gemini API for NLP and Document Drafting.
- Database: MongoDB Atlas (storing Users, Cases, and Chat Histories).
- Other Services: OCR engine.

--- Slide 9: Technologies to be used in the solution ---
- Backend Framework: Python, Flask

- AI/ML Services: Google Gemini AI
- Tools & Libraries: Flask-JWT-Extended (Auth), PyMongo, OCR APIs
- Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS
- Deployment: Cloud Hosting Platform
