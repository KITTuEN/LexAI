LEXAI: PROJECT OVERVIEW

1. ABSTRACT
NyayaVyavasth is an advanced AI-powered legal assistant designed to democratize access to legal information and streamline legal procedures for the general public. It uses NyayaVyavasth AI Pro to provide real-time consultations, automated document generation, and OCR analysis.

2. PROBLEM STATEMENT
India's legal system is complex and often inaccessible due to:
- Complicated legal jargon that confuses the layperson.
- High costs for initial legal advice.
- Language barriers for non-English/Hindi speakers.
- Massive documentation burdens and slow manual drafting.
- Lack of awareness regarding fundamental rights and applicable statutes.

3. SOLUTIONS
- Smart Case Helper: AI-driven analysis to predict applicable laws (IPC/BNS) and defense strategies.
- Full Multi-language Support: Dynamic translation into regional languages like Telugu and Hindi.
- Voice-enabled Interaction: Voice-to-text input supporting regional accents and languages.
- Automated Document Generation: Instant drafting of FIRs, notices, and agreements.
- OCR Digitization: Analyzing physical notices for immediate legal summaries.

4. OBJECTIVES
- To provide 24/7 affordable legal guidance to every citizen.
- To bridge the language gap in legal services via AI translation.
- To empower citizens with rights awareness and statutory knowledge.
- To streamline legal documentation for faster resolution.
- To assist users in preparing structured case summaries for professional legal consultation.
